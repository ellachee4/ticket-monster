Work Around the System

Alternative
Options

🏛️

In-Person Box Office

Look for in-person box offices or purchase tickets directly at the venue. Avoids all online service fees and queue systems entirely.

Example: Maggie Rogers' 2024 "Don't Forget Me" tour offered in-person box office sales.

🎲

Dice

Use Dice, an alternative ticketing platform to Ticketmaster. Known for upfront transparent pricing with no hidden service fees added at checkout.

Dice
🎫

Artist / Venue Direct

Many artists and independent venues sell tickets directly through their own websites, bypassing Ticketmaster and often at lower fees.

📻

Radio / Presale Giveaways

Local radio stations and artist fan clubs frequently run presale giveaways or offer direct-access presale codes before general tickets go on sale.

💳

Credit Card Presales

Certain credit cards (Amex, Citi, etc.) offer early access presale windows. Having the right card can get you tickets before they sell out in the general sale.

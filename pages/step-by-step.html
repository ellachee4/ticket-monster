Pre-Sale Checklist

Complete before the sale opens
  • Join artist mailing list/social media to find tour dates
  • When tour announced, set reminder on event page, allow text reminders
  • Sign up for presale events (venue, artist, credit card), save presale codes
  • Have credit card & address saved to Ticketmaster
  • Know exact number of tickets & max price you're willing to pay
  • Check ticket limits beforehand
  • Have as many people in your group join the sale simultaneously
  • Keep phone close, use (charged) laptop
  • Ensure secure internet connection (turn off VPN, avoid public WiFi)
  • Have presale code ready

During the Sale
1

Get Tech Ready

Keep your phone close and use a charged laptop. Usually easier to pick tickets on laptop.

2

Secure Your Connection

Ensure a secure internet connection. Turn off any VPN and avoid public WiFi, unstable connections can knock you out of queue.

3

Prepare Your Presale Code

Have your presale code copied and ready to paste. Don't scramble to find it when the sale opens. Example: THE123CODE567

4

Enter the Waiting Room ASAP

Join the waiting room 15-30 minutes before the sale opens. The earlier you enter, the better your queue position.

5

One Browser, One Tab

Use only one browser tab per concert date. Opening multiple tabs can reset your position or get you flagged. Coordinate with friends to each cover different dates.

6

Stay Alert & Don't Refresh

Once in the queue, do not refresh the page. Stay alert for your turn. Refreshing resets your queue position.

7

Select Tickets & Confirm

Choose your number of tickets, pick your seats quickly, and confirm payment. You have a time limit, don't deliberate too long or tickets will be released back.

8

You Did It!

Congratulations! You survived the Ticketmaster wars. Check your email for confirmation and note the delivery method for your tickets.
